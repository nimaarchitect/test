About MDC Architects

Focused from
start to finish.

MDC Architects, P.C. is an architectural, planning and interior design firm specializing in higher education projects. Based in Streamwood, Illinois, we bring decades of combined experience to every engagement.

We are singularly focused and personally involved in all facets of every project — from the first conceptual sketch to construction administration — because we believe that is what great architecture requires.

What We Do

Our practice.

Discuss Your Project
01
Architectural Design

Full-service architectural design from concept through construction documents and administration — for higher education, museums, research labs and institutional clients.

02
Planning & Interior Design

Programming, space planning and interior design that translates complex operational needs into environments that are functional, inspiring, and built to endure.

03
Feasibility Studies

Before a line is drawn, we examine site potential, programme fit and budget realism — giving you the clarity needed to make confident decisions from the outset.

04
Research Facilities

Highly technical laboratory environments for leading universities — designed with precision, flexibility, and safety standards that cutting-edge research demands.

05
Accessibility & Renovation

Code-compliant accessibility upgrades and thoughtful renovations that respect existing buildings while modernizing them for contemporary use.

06
Museum & Cultural

Gallery and museum environments that serve collections, visitors, and curators equally — balancing preservation requirements with powerful public experience.
